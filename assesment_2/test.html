Classic Pancake Recipe

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. n another bowl, whisk the egg, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and whisk until just combined.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle and pour batter to form pancakes.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip and cook until golden brown.
